What We Stand for

We believe in open source

We are all open-source evangelists and believe that open source will make deep learning technology accessible to everyone.

We are global

We have offices in Germany and China, and many of our teammates work remotely from around the world.

We stick to our values

We stand by our moral and ethical principles. Honesty, integrity and dependability are at the very core of everything we do.

We welcome fresh perspectives

We respect each other's unique cultures, beliefs, and qualities. We work towards creating a safe environment where everyone can thrive.

We encourage ownership

We support everyone in taking ownership of their work. Take initiative, stand up for your idea, and commit to its execution.

We work together as one

We cultivate a strong team spirit, working closely and transparently; Collaboration is essential, whether that’s inside, or between teams.

    Common Q&A

    What does your ideal teammate look like?
    We love to work with people who share our values and passion for open source and neural search. If you think you've got what it takes for our fast-changing, chaotic-but-fun, startup environment — apply away!
    How can I prepare for the interview?
    For engineering positions, we encourage you to make a pull request to our open-source projects. A good PR == a good cover letter! For other positions, tell us what you know about Jina, open source, deep learning, and search!
    What will my first day at Jina AI look like?
    You’ll receive a welcome email with all the instructions. If you are in one of our offices, we'll give you an office tour. There’ll be an onboarding session and welcome drinks with your future team lead and team mates.

Rationale 0.8 Update

Introducing Rationale 0.8: Make Better Decisions with Personalized Context Analysis! Experience improved outcomes analysis that considers the context of your decisions. Plus, a new persona system with up to 2000 letters of backstory, and UI improvements. Upgrade now!

Engineering Group
12 Feb 2023 • 2 min read

Rationale is a decision-making AI powered by the latest version of GPT-3 and in-context learning. It assists business owners, managers, and individuals in making tough decisions by generating a pros and cons list, SWOT analysis, multi-option analysis and causal chain analysis. By considering all relevant factors, Rationale helps users make rational decisions.

💡
Please force refresh the browser to see the latest change. This can be usually achieved by Hold ⇧ Shift (MacOS) or Ctrl (Windows/Linux) and click the Reload button.

We are excited to announce the release of Rationale 0.8, which comes packed with several new features and improvements to enhance your decision-making experience.

New persona system

With Rationale 0.8, you can now write up to 2000 letters of backstory for your persona, providing more in-depth and personalized analysis for your decisions. This feature allows Rationale to better understand the context of your decisions and provide more relevant and accurate analysis.

Causal chain analysis with context

Our outcomes analysis, also known as causal chain analysis, has been improved to consider the context of your decisions. With this feature, you can expect a more thorough and meaningful analysis of the potential outcomes of your decisions. Say goodbye to trivial outcomes and hello to more thoughtful and contextual analysis.

UI Improvements

We've made several improvements to the user interface on desktop and mobile, making it easier and more intuitive to use. In addition, we've addressed several bugs to ensure a seamless and stable experience for our users.

We hope you enjoy these new features and improvements in Rationale 0.8. As always, we welcome your feedback and suggestions for future updates.
